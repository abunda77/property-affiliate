## Generating Tracking Links

### Why Use Tracking Links?

Tracking links ensure you get credit for:
- Every visitor who clicks your link
- Every lead generated from your traffic
- All conversions within 30 days (cookie duration)

### How to Generate Links

1. **Navigate to Properties**:
   - Click **My Properties** in the sidebar
   - View all available properties

2. **Copy Your Link**:
   - Find the property you want to promote
   - Click **Copy Link Saya** button
   - Link is automatically copied to clipboard
   - Paste anywhere you want to share

### Link Format

Your tracking links look like:
```
https://yourdomain.com/p/luxury-villa-bali?ref=ABC12345
```

Where:
- `/p/luxury-villa-bali` = Property page
- `?ref=ABC12345` = Your unique affiliate code

### Alternative Referral Link

You can also use:
```
https://yourdomain.com/ref/ABC12345
```

This redirects to the property catalog with your tracking active.

### Where to Share Your Links

**Social Media**:
- Facebook posts and groups
- Instagram bio and stories
- Twitter/X posts
- LinkedIn articles
- TikTok video descriptions

**Messaging Apps**:
- WhatsApp status and chats
- Telegram channels
- LINE messages

**Other Channels**:
- Email newsletters
- Blog posts
- YouTube video descriptions
- Online forums

## Managing Leads

### Viewing Your Leads

1. Click **Leads** in the sidebar
2. See all leads assigned to you:
   - Visitor name
   - WhatsApp number
   - Property interested in
   - Status
   - Submission date
   - Your notes

### Understanding Lead Status

- **New**: Just submitted, needs immediate attention
- **Follow Up**: Initial contact made, schedule follow-up
- **Survey**: Property viewing arranged or completed
- **Closed**: Deal successfully closed! üéâ
- **Lost**: Lead did not convert

### Contacting Leads

**Via WhatsApp**:
1. Find the lead in your list
2. Click **Click to WA** button
3. WhatsApp Web opens with number pre-filled
4. Send your message

**First Contact Template**:
```
Halo [Name], terima kasih atas minat Anda pada [Property Name]. 
Saya [Your Name], siap membantu Anda. Kapan waktu yang tepat 
untuk saya menjelaskan detail properti ini?
```

### Updating Lead Status

1. Find the lead in your list
2. Click the **Status** dropdown
3. Select new status:
   - Moving to "Follow Up" after first contact
   - Moving to "Survey" when viewing scheduled
   - Moving to "Closed" when deal complete
   - Moving to "Lost" if lead doesn't convert
4. Status updates automatically

### Adding Notes

Track your conversations:
1. Click **Edit** on any lead
2. Add notes in the **Notes** field:
   - Call summaries
   - Viewing feedback
   - Objections
   - Next steps
3. Click **Save**

**Note Example**:
```
2025-11-23: First call, interested in 3BR option. 
Scheduled viewing for 2025-11-25 at 10 AM.
Budget confirmed at 2M.
```

### Lead Notifications

You receive WhatsApp notifications when:
- New lead submits contact form
- Lead is assigned to you
- Message format: "Halo, ada prospek baru atas nama [Name] untuk properti [Property]. Segera follow up!"

**Important**: Respond quickly! Fast response increases conversion rates.

**Leads**:

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: Not Receiving Lead Notifications

**Symptoms**: No WhatsApp messages when leads submit forms

**Solutions**:
1. Verify WhatsApp number in Profile Settings
2. Check number format includes country code (+62)
3. Ensure WhatsApp is active on that number
4. Check if messages are in spam/blocked
5. Contact admin if issue persists

### Issue: Tracking Links Not Working

**Symptoms**: Clicks not showing in dashboard

**Solutions**:
1. Verify you're using the correct link format
2. Don't modify the `?ref=` parameter
3. Test link in incognito/private browser
4. Ensure cookies are enabled
5. Wait a few minutes for data to update
6. Check if your account status is "Active"

### Issue: Cannot Access Dashboard

**Symptoms**: Login fails or access denied

**Solutions**:
1. Verify account is approved (check email)
2. Ensure status is "Active" (not Blocked)
3. Reset password if needed
4. Clear browser cache and cookies
5. Try different browser
6. Contact admin for account status

### Issue: Promotional Materials Won't Download

**Symptoms**: Download button doesn't work

**Solutions**:
1. Check internet connection
2. Try different browser
3. Disable popup blockers
4. Clear browser cache
5. Contact admin if property has no images

### Issue: Low Conversion Rate

**Symptoms**: Many clicks but few leads

**Solutions**:
1. **Improve Response Time**: Reply to leads faster
2. **Better Targeting**: Share with more relevant audiences
3. **Quality Content**: Use better images and descriptions
4. **Clear CTA**: Make it easy to contact you
5. **Build Trust**: Complete your profile, be professional
6. **Follow Up**: Don't give up after first contact

### Getting Help

If you need assistance:

1. **Check This Guide**: Most answers are here
2. **Contact Admin**: Use the support contact provided
3. **Provide Details**:
   - Your affiliate code

   - Screenshots if possible

## Tips for Success

### Marketing Tips

1. **Be Consistent**: Post regularly, not just once
2. **Use Stories**: Instagram/WhatsApp stories are powerful
3. **Engage**: Respond to comments and questions
4. **Be Authentic**: Share genuine enthusiasm
5. **Add Value**: Provide helpful information, not just links

3. **Listen First**: Understand their needs before pitching

4. **Prioritize**: Focus on hot leads first
